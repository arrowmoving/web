Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my move? +

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (May-September). For last-minute moves, please call us directly, and we will do our best to accommodate you.

Are you licensed and insured? +

Yes, Arrow Moving Van Lines is a fully licensed and insured moving company. We carry comprehensive liability insurance to ensure your peace of mind throughout the moving process.

Do you provide packing materials? +

Yes, we offer professional packing services and provide all necessary materials, including high-quality boxes, bubble wrap, packing paper, and tape. These can be included in your moving package or purchased separately.

How is the cost of my move calculated? +

Our pricing is transparent and based on factors such as the volume of your belongings, the distance of the move, and any additional services required. We provide a detailed, free estimate with no hidden fees.
